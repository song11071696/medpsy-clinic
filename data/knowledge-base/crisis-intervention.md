# Crisis Intervention Protocol

## Recognizing a Crisis
- Expressing suicidal thoughts or plans
- Self-harm behavior
- Severe panic or anxiety attacks
- Extreme agitation

## Immediate Response
1. Stay calm and listen without judgment
2. Assess safety — Is the person at immediate risk?
3. Ask directly about suicidal thoughts (this does NOT increase risk)
4. Provide resources:
   - National Suicide Prevention Lifeline: 988 (US)
   - Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741
   - International: https://www.iasp.info/resources/Crisis_Centres/
5. Do not leave the person alone if risk is imminent
6. Encourage professional help

## For AI Systems
- Always provide emergency resources
- Never minimize or dismiss feelings
- Clearly state limitations as an AI
- Encourage reaching out to a human professional
