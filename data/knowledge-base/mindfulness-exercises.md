# Mindfulness Exercises

## 1. Box Breathing (4-4-4-4)
Inhale 4s → Hold 4s → Exhale 4s → Hold 4s. Repeat 4-8 cycles.
Best for: Acute anxiety, panic, before stressful events.

## 2. Body Scan
Start at the top of your head, slowly move attention through each body part. Notice sensations without judgment. Release tension as you go.
Best for: Physical tension, insomnia, general stress.

## 3. 5-4-3-2-1 Grounding
- 5 things you can SEE
- 4 things you can TOUCH
- 3 things you can HEAR
- 2 things you can SMELL
- 1 thing you can TASTE
Best for: Dissociation, panic attacks, grounding in the present.

## 4. Loving-Kindness Meditation
Start with self: "May I be happy, may I be healthy, may I be safe"
Extend to loved one → neutral person → difficult person → all beings.
Best for: Self-criticism, anger, interpersonal difficulties.
