# MEDICAL_DISCLAIMER.md - MedPsy Clinic Medical Disclaimer

## Important Notice

**MedPsy Clinic is NOT a medical device, clinical tool, or professional mental health service.**

This platform is a **rule-based mental health knowledge retrieval and consultation aid** designed for educational and informational purposes only.

---

## What MedPsy Clinic IS

- A knowledge retrieval system that searches psychology literature and provides informational responses
- A demonstration of privacy-preserving AI technology applied to mental health content
- An educational tool for understanding CBT concepts, anxiety management, and other psychological topics
- A platform that includes crisis keyword detection to surface relevant hotline information

## What MedPsy Clinic IS NOT

- ❌ A substitute for professional psychological or psychiatric assessment
- ❌ A diagnostic tool — it does not diagnose mental health conditions
- ❌ A treatment provider — it does not prescribe or recommend specific treatments
- ❌ A crisis intervention service — crisis detection is keyword-based and may miss cases or produce false alarms
- ❌ A replacement for emergency services (call 120/911 for immediate danger)
- ❌ A certified medical device under any regulatory framework (FDA, CE, NMPA, etc.)

## Limitations of AI-Generated Content

- Responses are based on pre-loaded knowledge documents and may not reflect the latest clinical research
- AI responses cannot account for individual medical history, medications, or co-occurring conditions
- Crisis detection relies on keyword matching and natural language patterns — it is not a clinical assessment
- The system may not detect all crisis situations (false negatives) and may flag non-crisis content (false positives)

## User Responsibility

By using MedPsy Clinic, you acknowledge that:

1. You will not rely on this platform as your sole source of mental health guidance
2. You will consult a licensed mental health professional for clinical concerns
3. You understand that AI-generated responses are informational, not clinical advice
4. You will contact emergency services (120/911) if you are in immediate danger
5. You are responsible for your own health decisions

## Deployment Responsibility

Organizations deploying MedPsy Clinic must:

- Clearly display this disclaimer to all users before they interact with the platform
